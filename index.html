Hotels by region

Prices are the live Expedia total for 2 separate rooms — Room A: 2 adults + your 17-year-old, Room B: 2 adults + your 11-year-old — for July 2026, shown in CAD with USD in parentheses. Each room's price was identical regardless of the kid's age, so these are real, matched per-hotel totals, not estimates.

📌Update — searching one room for all 6 of you returned zero Jasper townsite hotels. Splitting it into 2 rooms found real availability right in town (Jasper Inn & Suites) plus a second option inside the park near Miette. Worth remembering generally: if a destination looks sold out, try searching smaller room splits before assuming it's unavailable. A Hinton hotel (~70km/1hr outside the park) is included as a budget backup only.
📌Also — Fairmont Banff Springs, Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise, and Fairmont Jasper Park Lodge are all sold out for these dates. They're removed from the list below; the best remaining splurge options are Buffalo Mountain Lodge (Banff) and Miette Mountain Cabins (Jasper). Worth checking Fairmont directly in case of late cancellations.
