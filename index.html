Day by day

The full 14 days

Jul 19 – Aug 1, 2026. Two nights Calgary → four nights Canmore → two nights Golden → four nights Hinton → one night Calgary → fly home.

📍
Icefields Parkway: You do NOT need a separate day trip from Banff. The full Parkway (Peyto Lake, Columbia Icefield, Glacier Skywalk, Sunwapta Falls) is naturally covered on Day 9 (Jul 27) as you drive from Golden north to Jasper via Lake Louise and Hwy 93.
🎫
Already booked — Day 4 (Jul 22): Full day tour covering Johnston Canyon, Lake Louise, Moraine Lake & Emerald Lake. Pickup at 9:30 AM at the Travel Alberta Canmore Visitor Information Centre, 2801 Bow Valley Trail, Canmore, AB T1W 3A2.
